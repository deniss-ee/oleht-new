Clear Space

Always maintain a minimum clear space around the logo equal to the cap-height of the "Õ" character. No other visual elements may enter this zone.

1× cap-height 1× cap-height 1× 1×
Õhtuleht

The clear space zone is defined by the cap-height of the wordmark. On a 32px logo, this equals approximately 8px on all sides.

Dos & Don'ts

✓ Do
✓ Use the approved logo files (SVG or high-res PNG) without modification.
✓ Use on backgrounds with sufficient contrast — white, black, or brand red only.
✓ Maintain the minimum clear space zone on all sides.
✓ Use the logomark as a standalone icon where space is limited.
✓ Scale proportionally, holding the Shift key when resizing.
✗ Don't
✗ Do not stretch, skew, or distort the logo in any direction.
✗ Do not recolour the logo outside approved colour variants.
✗ Do not place the logo on busy, low-contrast backgrounds.
✗ Do not add drop shadows, gradients, or other effects.
✗ Do not alter the letter-spacing or typeface of the wordmark.
✗ Do not use an outdated or unofficial version of the logo.

Editorial Typography & Article Layout

Rules for long-form journalism. These specifications ensure maximum readability, accessibility, and brand consistency across all article pages.

Reading Experience

Reading Column

The optimal reading column width is the single most important layout decision for long-form text. Too wide creates fatiguing saccades; too narrow feels cramped.

Column Width Specification

Eesti päevaleht Õhtuleht pakub lugejatele põhjalikku reportaaži ja analüüsi. Teksti veerus on optimaalne ridade laius 60–75 tähemärki, mis tagab mugava lugemiskogemuse. See standardveerg on täpselt 680 pikselit lai — piisavalt lai, et mahutada viis–kuus sõna reas, kuid mitte nii lai, et silmad väsivad.

Rich Text

Rich Text Elements

Styled elements within article body copy — quotes, lists, captions, drop caps, and inline links.

Drop Cap

Applied to the first paragraph of a feature or long-form article using the .has-dropcap class. Only use for editorial features — not news briefs.

Eesti poliitika on viimastel kuudel läbinud kiired muutused. Uue valitsuskoalitsiooni moodustamine tõi kaasa ootamatuid nihkeid nii majandus- kui ka sotsiaalpoliitikas, millest paljud kodanikud veel täielikult aru ei saa.

Teine lõik jätkab tavapärases kirjastiilis, ilma suurtäheta. Drop cap rakendatakse ainult esimesele lõigule.

Accessibility & Contrast

All editorial text must meet WCAG 2.1 AA as a minimum. Long-form body copy should target AAA. Contrast ratios are calculated against the standard white background.

Standard: Body text, headings, and interactive elements must meet WCAG 2.1 AA (4.5:1 for normal text, 3:1 for large text). Long-form articles should target AAA (7:1).
