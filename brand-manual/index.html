Dos & Don'ts

✓ Do
✓ Use approved logo files without modification.
✓ Use on backgrounds with sufficient contrast.
✓ Maintain minimum clear space zone.
✓ Scale proportionally only.
✗ Don't
✗ Do not stretch, skew, or distort.
✗ Do not recolour outside approved variants.
✗ Do not place on busy, low-contrast backgrounds.
✗ Do not add drop shadows, gradients, or effects.
✗ Do not alter spacing or typeface of the wordmark.
